Help Neil Roy Find a Kidney Donor

A remarkable father, husband, and friend from Louisville, Kentucky needs your compassion

Neil Roy lights up every room he enters. Known for his generous spirit and unwavering kindness, this beloved Louisville resident has touched countless lives through his dedication to family and community.

Today, Neil faces a health challenge that only the compassion of another person can solve. His kidneys are asking for help, and a living donor could be the answer to his family's prayers.

A living kidney donation would give Neil the opportunity to return to the vibrant, active life he loves—watching his children grow, supporting his wife, and continuing to be the extraordinary man everyone admires.

We are specifically looking for someone with B+ blood group who has no current medical conditions or health issues to ensure the safety and well-being of both Neil and the donor.

That's why we're reaching out to our extended community. Sometimes the greatest gift we can give is a part of ourselves. Your consideration of becoming Neil's donor is already a beautiful act of humanity, regardless of where the journey leads you.

4
Share Neil's story – Even if you can't donate, sharing this page might help find someone who can.
